THE ORIGIN STORY

Tag’d started with a simple observation, business cards don’t work the way they used to.
They’re handed out once, forgotten quickly, and create clutter for the very people they’re meant to help.

There had to be a better way to share information instantly without awkwardly exchanging phones or relying on platform-locked tools like Apple-only tap sharing. TAG'D was built as a universal solution; A faster, cleaner way to connect that works across devices and across industries.

What began as a smarter alternative to paper business cards quickly revealed something bigger: the same technology could power tools for creators, technicians, businesses, and everyday professionals who need reliable ways to connect their physical world to their digital identity.

FROM ANALOG GRIT TO DIGITAL FINESSE

We don't believe in "tech." We believe in Utility. Our journey wasn't about coding apps; it was about machining the physical bridge between your physical workspace and your digital identity. We replaced paper business cards with precision-made anchors.

THE MIND BEHIND THE MACHINE

"I was tired of having to lug around countless cards where it felt like I was babysitting pieces of fragile paper with each business encounter. I wanted something that felt like a tool—something with weight, something with purpose. Tagd USA isn't just a business; it's a statement against planned obsolescence."

As a designer and engineer, I spent years observing the embarassment of either not having a business card or that the card hard warped from being inside a wallet. I wanted to build something that could survive a fall from a crane and still work exactly as it needed to in that critical moment.
