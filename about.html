THE ORIGIN STORY

It started with a broken hinge and a lost file. The digital world promised freedom, but gave us plastic dongles and fragile connections. Tagd USA was born in a garage in the Midwest with one goal: to fuse the permanence of industrial manufacturing with the speed of the digital future.

FROM ANALOG GRIT TO DIGITAL STEEL

We don't believe in "tech." We believe in Utility. Our journey wasn't about coding apps; it was about machining the physical bridge between your physical workspace and your digital identity. We replaced paper business cards with precision-milled anchors.
